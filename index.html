Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your cookies stay fresh? +

Our cookies stay fresh for up to 7 days when stored in an airtight container at room temperature. For extended freshness, you can freeze them for up to 2 weeks.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options? +

Yes! We offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan cookies. Please check our products page or contact us for our special dietary options.

What's your return policy? +

We guarantee 100% satisfaction. If you're not happy with your order, we'll replace it or offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

Do you deliver internationally? +

Currently, we deliver within the local area and select nearby regions. For international inquiries, please contact us directly for special arrangements.

Can I customize my cookie order? +

Absolutely! We love customization. Contact us with your special requests for personalized flavors, packaging, or bulk orders.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. For security, all transactions are encrypted and processed securely.
